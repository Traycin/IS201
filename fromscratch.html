Welcome to the World of Golf

Discover the beauty and challenges of golf, from lush greens to the strategic play.

  1. Golf Basics
    • There are 18 hole rounds that are played by each golfer. The player with the lowest score wins the round.

    • Each time a club is swung to strike the ball it is called a stroke and these strokes are added up to create what was referred to as a score

    • You are allowed 14 clubs in the bag during tournament play

  2. Advanced Techniques
    • The best way to play is to hit it straight down the fairway, which is the regulation "bounds" of the hole

    • In order to play well one must practice often and work each aspect of the game, from short putts to long drives it is all important.

    • After one has been playing a long time they may aquire their own sense of play and create a personal stragety to win the game.
